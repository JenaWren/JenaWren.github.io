Dessert: Apple Crisp

Home Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

This is my mom's apple crisp recipe. It always makes the house smell like fall and warms the spirit.

Ingredients

  • 6-8 apples peeled and sliced
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup gluten free flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 6 tbsp butter

Instructions

  • Preheat your oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  • Place the peeled and sliced items in a baking dish, adding water
  • Blend the sugar, flour, cinnamon and butter in a seperate bowl
  • Bake for 45 minutes
  • For an extra delicious dessert, add a scoop of vanilla ice cream!
