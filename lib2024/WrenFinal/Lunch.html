Lunch: Strawberry Balsamic Spinach Salad

Home Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

This is another recipe of my grandmother's that is perfect for the summertime. Super easy to put together and a refreshing midday meal.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups spinach leaves
  • 2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1 cup orange slices
  • Crumbled feta cheese (as much as you'd like)
  • 1 cup balsamic vinaigrette
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • Handful of walnuts (optional)

Instructions

  • Place spinach, strawberries, orange and feta in a large bowl
  • Toss with balsamic vinaigrette and pepper
