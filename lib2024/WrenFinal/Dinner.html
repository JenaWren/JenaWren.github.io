Dinner: Irish Stew

Home Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

This is my grandmother's recipe for traditional Irish stew. It's the perfect meal for a cold winter night and makes plenty of servings which is great for leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb stew beef
  • 1 cup celery
  • 1 cup carrots
  • 1 cup potatoes
  • 1 cup onions
  • 1 cup V8 juice
  • 2 tbsp minute tapioca
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Instructions

  • Preheat your oven to 250 degrees Farenheit
  • Cut the beef, celery, carrots, potatoes and onions into bite-size pieces
  • Combine all ingredients into a large dutch oven, making sure that the juice, tapioca, salt and sugar are well mixed
  • Cover tightly and place in the oven for 4 hours
  • IMPORTANT: do not stir while cooking
