Breakfast: Banana Bread

Home Breakfast Lunch Dinner Dessert

This recipe was my grandmothers and makes a really great breakfast that will last the week. It is easy to make gluten free by simply substituting flour for gluten free flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 1/2 cup gluten free flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda

Instructions

  • Preheat your oven to 350 degrees Farenheit and line a loaf pan with parchment paper
  • Mix sugar, butter and eggs in a large bowl
  • Add two bananas to the mixture and mash
  • In another bowl, combine gluten free flour and baking soda
  • Combine the dry mixture with the wet mixture
  • Pour the mixture into the loaf pan and bake for an hour
